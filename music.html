This is my music website.

On this website, I share with you my musical journey so far and recommend great resources that have helped me along the way.

How it started for me.

I loved to sing when I was very much young, say from age 3. However, by the age of 8, I started leaning more towards musical instruments. I believe the toy musical instruments my Dad bought for my brother and I triggered it. It began with a passion for the keyboard. I just loved the arrangement of the keys and loved watching live bands play. My journey hasn't been easy particularly because I hardly had any tutors to take me through a clear structure of learning so I did most of the work on my own. Years down the line, I have learned a little bit of the drums and bass guitar.

Some key individuals who have been my direct tutors along my journey are:

  • Cassey
  • Isaac
  • Prince
  • Ephraim
  • Osei
